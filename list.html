To show I love you, I'll

- Complement you

- Be honest with you

- Support you

- Keep up with you

- Talk to you every single day

- Make food for you

- Take you out on more than just dates

- Ask for your advice

- Protect you, physically and emotionally

- Let you pick.

- Keep you company.

- Appreciate you

After I lose you, I will

- Eat

- Look at memories of us

- Lock away or destroy memorabilia (depending on what you meant to me)

- Drown my sorrows in music, books, and movies.

- Cry

- Share stories of us

- Write

- Look forward not back

- Learn from my mistakes

- (Try to) let go

- Forgive everyone and everything including myself
